Action Time. Stand up. Sit Down. Turn around. Raise your hands. Hands down. Hands up. Turn Left. Turn right. Raise your left hand and shake it. Raise your right hand and shake it. Jump. Stand on the left foot. Hop. Stop. Stand on the right foot. Hop. Stop.

Fall in line, everybody. Stand at ease. Attention. Forward march. Put your left foot forward. Put your right foot forward. Left, right, left, right, left, right. Halt. About turn. Left, right, left, right, left, right. Halt.
